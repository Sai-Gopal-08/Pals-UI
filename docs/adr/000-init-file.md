## Reason for this folder to exist

### Abbreviation: 
ADR - Architecture Decision Record

### Purpose: 
- To document the architectural decisions made during the development of the project. 
- This helps in maintaining a clear record of why certain decisions were made, which can be useful for future reference.

### Do not:
- Do not include trivial decisions that do not have a significant impact on the architecture.
- Do not include decisions that are not related to the architecture of the project.

### Do:
- Include decisions that have a impact on the architecture of the project.
- Include the rationale behind each decision, as well as any alternatives that were considered.
